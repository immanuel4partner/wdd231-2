Meet The Medical Director

Dr Amajuoyi Conel P.G.D., M.A., M.B.B.C.H.

Dr. Amajuoyi as the Chief Medical Director of a Paediatrics and Gynaecology Consulting: Dr. Amajuoyi is a highly respected and internationally recognized medical expert in paediatrics and gynaecology, with over 30 years of clinical experience. As the Chief Medical Director of a renowned Paediatrics and Gynaecology Consulting practice, Dr. Amajuoyi is known for an unwavering commitment to patient care and medical excellence. Her passion for women's and children's health is matched by a deep dedication to continuous learning and professional development. Over the years, she has earned multiple advanced degrees and fellowships, served on the faculty of several medical institutions, and delivered lectures across more than 90 countries, mentoring and guiding healthcare professionals around the world. .
